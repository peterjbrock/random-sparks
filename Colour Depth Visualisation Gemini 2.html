Stacked Color Bit Depth Visualizer

Compare bit depths side-by-side. Observe how doubling the color resolution with each added bit progressively smooths the color gradients and removes banding.

Teaching Tip: Highlight how 1-bit starts with just 2 choices (binary 0 or 1). By scaling variants through structural exponential steps (2n), 8-bit resolution reaches 256 variations, making the color appear seamless to human perception.
